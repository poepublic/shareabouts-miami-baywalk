{{#_}}About the Miami DDA{{/_}}

{{#_}}The vision of the Miami DDA is to grow, strengthen and promote the economic health/vitality of Downtown Miami. As an independent agency of the City, the Miami DDA advocates, facilitates, plans and executes business development, planning, capital improvements and marketing & communication strategies.{{/_}}
